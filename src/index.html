How to Use

Jimmy Eat World displays recipes from all over the world. Click on a dot to view recipes from that country. You can click on the recipe's picture or title to view more information on that recipe. If you decide you enjoy a recipe you can click on Add to Shopping List. Then, the recipe ingredients will be added to your own Shopping List.
